About Aniket Sikhana Prasikhkhana Seva Sansthan

We are a non-profit organization dedicated to making a positive impact in communities worldwide.

Founded in 2005, Aniket Sikhana Prasikhkhana Seva Sansthan has been at the forefront of humanitarian efforts, providing essential resources, education, and healthcare to those in need. Our mission is to create sustainable change through community-driven initiatives.

  • Transparency: We ensure 90% of donations directly support our programs.
  • Impact: Our projects are designed for long-term, sustainable change.
  • Community: We work closely with local communities to understand their needs.
  • Global Reach: Operating in over 20 countries across 4 continents.
