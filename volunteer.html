Volunteer FAQ

Common questions about volunteering with Aniket Sikhana Prasikhkhana Seva Sansthan.

We offer flexible volunteering opportunities to accommodate different schedules. You can volunteer for as little as a few hours per month or commit to regular weekly shifts. Some roles require specific time commitments, which will be clearly communicated during the application process.

While some roles benefit from specific skills, many volunteer positions require only enthusiasm and a willingness to help. We provide training for all volunteers and match individuals with opportunities that align with their interests and abilities.

Yes! We welcome volunteers of all ages, though some positions may have age requirements. For volunteers under 18, we require parental consent and may have additional guidelines to ensure a safe and positive experience.

Yes, we offer various remote volunteering options, particularly for roles involving professional skills like graphic design, writing, social media management, and virtual tutoring. These opportunities allow you to contribute from anywhere.

All volunteers receive orientation and role-specific training. You'll have a designated supervisor for guidance and support, and we provide any necessary materials or resources. We also offer ongoing training opportunities and recognize volunteer contributions regularly.
