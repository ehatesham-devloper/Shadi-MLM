Our Story

Founded in 2005, Aniket Sikhana Prasikhkhana Seva Sansthan began as a small community initiative and has grown into an international organization with impact across four continents.

Our journey started when our founder, Sarah Thompson, witnessed the devastating effects of poverty during a volunteer trip to Southeast Asia. Determined to make a difference, she gathered a team of like-minded individuals who shared her vision of creating sustainable change through community empowerment.

Over the years, we've expanded our reach while maintaining our core values of transparency, accountability, and community-centered approaches. Today, we operate in over 20 countries, working hand-in-hand with local communities to address their most pressing needs.

Our Mission, Vision & Values

Our Mission

To empower underserved communities through sustainable development programs in education, healthcare, and economic opportunity, creating lasting positive change.

Our Vision

A world where every person has access to quality education, healthcare, and the opportunity to reach their full potential, regardless of their circumstances.

Our Values

Integrity, compassion, collaboration, innovation, and accountability guide everything we do, ensuring we create meaningful and sustainable impact.

Our Journey

2005

Foundation

Aniket Sikhana Prasikhkhana Seva Sansthan was founded with a focus on education initiatives in rural communities.

2008

Expansion to Healthcare

We expanded our programs to include healthcare access, starting mobile medical clinics.

2012

International Reach

Began operations in Southeast Asia and Latin America, establishing our first international offices.

2016

Sustainable Development Focus

Shifted focus to sustainable development programs that create long-term community resilience.

2020

COVID-19 Response

Launched emergency response programs to support communities affected by the pandemic.

2023

Current Operations

Operating in 20+ countries with 85 completed projects and over 25,000 people helped.
